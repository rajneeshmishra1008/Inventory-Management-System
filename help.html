🔐 How to Login into Inventory System? +
Step 1: Open the Login Page from the navbar.
Step 2: Enter your registered email or username.
Step 3: Type your password carefully.
Step 4: Click on the Login button.
Step 5: If login is successful, session storage activates automatically and redirects you to Dashboard.
Troubleshooting: If password is incorrect, reset your credentials from Settings or contact administrator support.
📦 How to Add Inventory Product? +
Step 1: Go to Inventory Page.
Step 2: Click on "Add Product".
Step 3: Enter Product Name, Quantity, Price and Category.
Step 4: Upload product image if required.
Step 5: Click Save Product.
Result: Product instantly appears in inventory table and history logs.
📊 How Dashboard Analytics Work? +
Step 1: Dashboard fetches inventory data from localStorage/database.
Step 2: Total items, low stock and sales analytics are calculated.
Step 3: Graphs and cards update automatically.
Step 4: Recent activities appear in Activity History section.
Professional Tip: Use charts and category filters for faster stock analysis.
⚙ How to Enable Dark Mode? +
Step 1: Open Settings Page.
Step 2: Click on Theme Toggle.
Step 3: Dark mode gets saved permanently using localStorage.
Step 4: Reload page to verify settings persistence.
